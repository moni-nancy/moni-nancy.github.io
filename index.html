Morning Meditation Techniques

Morning is often considered to be the best time to meditate, since the mind is quiet and fresh. Most of us are also less likely to doze off in the early hours. People who practice every day appreciate morning meditation since it sets a calm and productive tone before the day's activities and distractions begin.

Starting your day with a morning meditation can gently release grogginess and help set the conditions for a more successful day. Whether you wake up at 5am or 10am, incorporating a daily wake-up meditation can become your anchor in a day full of often unpredictable situations and variables.
